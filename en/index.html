The House

A real neighbourhood brasserie in Paris 12

At La Terrasse Bercy, the décor speaks for itself: deep red, leather banquettes, polished zinc, dark wood, brass details. Everything is designed to feel like a long-time friend's place — except here, you come for the morning coffee and stay until the last cocktail of the night.

Homemade French cuisine

Traditional brasserie menu, daily specials simmered on site, seasonal seafood platters. Everything is prepared in-house with ingredients chosen for their flavour, not their convenience.

From morning coffee to last call

Open from 6am to 2am, every day of the week. Breakfast, lunch, afternoon coffee, aperitif, dinner and a last drink — a whole neighbourhood passing through the same red awning.

The spirit of Paris 12

Locals on lunch break, travellers between two trains, fans before a concert: the brasserie is a meeting point, a landmark, a place that lives all day long.

A wide menu, simple choices

Starters, French classics, salads, fish, meats, daily set menus, snacks and homemade desserts. The full menu is available on site or by phone at +33 1 43 07 04 07.

Terrace & Bar

Paris 12's favourite after-work spot

As soon as the sun comes out, the wide terrace of La Terrasse Bercy becomes one of the most coveted spots in the neighbourhood. With friends, after work, between two trains at Gare de Lyon — this is where you order the first drink, and very often the last one too.

Wide sun-drenched terrace

A truly Parisian terrace, generous, shaded by plane trees, open from the very first warm days of spring until late in the season.

Cocktails & after-work

Spritz, mojito, signature cocktails, French wines by the glass, draft beers: a bar menu designed to make the apéro last well into the night.

Seasonal seafood platters

When the season allows, the brasserie brings out the oysters, shrimps, whelks and shared plates — Parisian tradition in its simplest form.

Frequently asked

Everything you usually want to know

Where to eat near Accor Arena before a concert?

La Terrasse Bercy is a 5-minute walk from Accor Arena, at 102 rue de Bercy. The brasserie is open from 6am to 2am, 7 days a week — the ideal address for dinner before the show, and a last drink after.

Is there an outdoor terrace?

Yes. La Terrasse Bercy features one of the largest Parisian terraces in the 12th arrondissement, opened from the first warm days of spring, ideal for sunny lunches, after-work drinks or outdoor dinners.

Can I book a private space for an event?

Yes. Several private spaces are available for birthdays, business lunches, cocktails or after-work events. To check exact capacities and availability, please call the brasserie at +33 1 43 07 04 07.

What are the opening hours?

The brasserie is open 7 days a week, from 6:00 am to 2:00 am. From breakfast to the very last drink.

How do I get there?

The address is 102 rue de Bercy, Paris 12. Bercy metro (lines 6 and 14) is 2 minutes away, Gare de Lyon (lines 1, 14, RER A and D) is an 8-minute walk. Both Gare de Bercy and Gare de Lyon are right next door.

Are there vegetarian options?

The menu features starters, salads and meat-free mains. Exact options may change with the market and the season — the safest bet is to call ahead at +33 1 43 07 04 07.
